About me

I received my B.S. degree in Statistics from Central South University in 2022. Now, I am pursuing my M.S. degree and advised by Lecturer.Shanshan Luo and Prof.Zhi Geng in Statistics at Beijing Technology and Business University (expected graduation in 2026.06). My research primarily focuses on causal inference, with particular emphasis on causal effect identification and estimation in the presence of unmeasured confounding.
